Welcome to the Wildlife Sanctuary!

We are dedicated to the conservation and protection of wildlife. Explore our site to learn more about the animals we care for and how you can get involved. Thank you for visiting!

Our Featured Animals

White-tailed Deer

The most common species in our sanctuary, these graceful creatures can be spotted grazing in our meadows at dawn and dusk.

Great Horned Owl

Our nocturnal residents can be heard hooting throughout the forest. Join our night tours to catch a glimpse of these majestic birds.

Red Fox

Known for their cunning and beauty, our fox family has made their home in the northern section of the sanctuary.
